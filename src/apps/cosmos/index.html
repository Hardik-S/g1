Cosmos

Navigate a to-scale (×10-9) solar system rendered with Three.js and velocity Verlet integration. Adjust time, gravity, and orbital trails to watch planets evolve in real time.

  • Newtonian physics with velocity Verlet integration.
  • Teleport the camera to any planet instantly.
  • Adjust gravity strength and orbital trails live.

Mouse

Left drag
Orbit around the focused body.
Right drag
Pan the camera laterally through space.
Scroll
Zoom in or out to inspect the system.

Keyboard

R
Reset the camera to the default solar view.

Control panel

  • Use Time speed to fast-forward or slow the simulation.
  • Adjust Gravity to exaggerate or mellow orbital motion.
  • Toggle Show trails to reveal each body's orbit history.
  • Open Camera to teleport to any planet or reset the view.
